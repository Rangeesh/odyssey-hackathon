About LYRA

LYRA (LYRics Animated) is an AI-powered music video generator that brings your favorite songs to life.

How It Works

The Process

  1. Search & Fetch: We use the LRCLIB API to find time-synced lyrics for your chosen song.
  2. Image Generation: Google Gemini creates artistic illustrations for each segment based on the lyrics and mood.
  3. Video Animation: Odyssey AI animates these static images into dynamic video clips.
  4. Stitching: All video segments are combined into a seamless music video.

Technologies Used

  • Django (Backend)
  • Google Gemini (Image Generation)
  • Odyssey AI (Video Generation)
  • LRCLIB (Lyrics Database)
  • MoviePy (Video Processing)
